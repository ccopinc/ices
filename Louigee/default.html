The best things in life taste great. Louie G. learned this lesson from his family and by following it strictly, and using family proven recipes, the excellence of his ices and ice creams have been maintained over the years and endorsed by the establishment of over 45 franchises in eight separate states. And as the ever growing number of highly satisfied customers and the continuing happy success of our franchises attest, there’s no stopping a good, and mouth-watering, treat!

We are the only Uncle Louigie Gourment Italian Ice in Myrtle Beach, SC. Our products have been made in New York for over 20 years.
